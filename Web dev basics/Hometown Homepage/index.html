Visit Valparaiso, IN

Enjoy this vibrant town only one hour away from Chicago.

Top activities to do in Valparaiso

Go ice skating downtown

The Central Park Plaza Ice Rink is a great winter activity.

Attend a show or a concert

Enjoy a night with some music at the Northwest Health Amphitheater.

Have fun at Zao Island

Visit Zao Island for an arcade, mini golf, go karting, and more!

Watch a movie at the 49er Drive-In

Enjoy watching a movie at a drive-in theatre.
